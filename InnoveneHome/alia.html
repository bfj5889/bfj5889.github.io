Alia Kozlova

Experienced business leader with a talent for creating new business functions, developing high performance teams in the areas of consumer insights, business information, strategic and operational planning and strategy. Strong track record in big business projects, including running own business and investments. Used to work in challenging business environments (geopolitically complex countries, price wars), leading people through change (business transformation). Hard working, ambitious and goal orientated leader with strong drive towards business results and personal growth; multilingual with substantial working experience in various European markets in big multinational companies. Flexible, visionary, with strong views, but with the capacity to listen and be realistic.

Currently works as a business consultant in InReach Global Consulting, a consulting company based in UK. Previously pursued an ex-patriot career in a big multinational company (BAT, one of the leading companies in its business sector - more than 57,000 people worldwide, contributing approximately £30 billion to governments worldwide in excise and other taxes in 2014). Alia has started from the Head of Marketing Research and Information Department on an end market level (Russia) progressing to Director for Business Information at a regional level (Eastern Europe, Middle East and Africa). Her interim positions were covering market intelligence, strategic and operational planning, project office, knowledge management in various countries in Europe. At some stage Alia was a European Coordinator for Anti Illicit Trade research, later - a Global Insights Coordinator for Vogue brand, was leading a resource allocation planning project across 11 countries and all business functions (resulting in creation of 10-year strategic plans for all markets and business functions, optimising budget allocation across 11 end markets).

Earlier in her career Alia was a General Manager of CIS subdivision MEMRB International, the leading international marketing research agency, a business owned by the third President of The Republic of Cyprus George Vasiliou. It was a startup business in Russia which Alia successfully expanded to seven more countries of Eastern Europe, setting up the business, hiring and developing high performing teams to service demanding multinationals (Proctor&Gamble, Nestle, PepsiCo, Danone, Ford) and advertising agencies (Saatchi&Saatchi, Leo Burnett).

Alia graduated from St. Petersburg University, MA in linguistics (Roman languages and literature), and then a PHD in psychology from Leningrad State University.
