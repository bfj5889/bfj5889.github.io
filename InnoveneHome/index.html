About Innovene

Innovene is a drug discovery and development company based in New York City working with Northwell Health, the City University of New York, and Ohio State Medical Center. The company operates as an incubator for innovative research to develop a new class of therapeutics derived from plant compounds. Innovene is now poised to introduce its proprietary botanic drug TriCurin into the cancer prevention and treatment marketplace, targeting the human papillomavirus (HPV) and the associated cancers.

Innovene is developing a new class of therapeutics derived from GRAS compounds (GRAS = Generally Recognized as Safe, as designated by the FDA), was founded by two physician researchers Mario Castellanos, M.D. & Anita Szerszen, D.O., inventors in life sciences sector and serial entrepreneurs.
