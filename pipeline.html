Clinical development program for TriCurin will first examine the effects of intravaginal cream in women with chronic HPV infection. Phase I clinical trial can begin late fall 2017 with the acquisition of further funding. It is anticipated we can obtain clinical results in 15 months. Upon completion, we will initiate a Phase II trial that will require 18 months to complete.

Pipelines



TriCurin

TriCurin is composed of plant derived components designated as GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe) by the FDA. TriCurin inhibits the expression of the viral oncoproteins E6 &E7, which is essential for the proliferation of HPV-infected cells, and increases the expression of P53 & RB, potent suppressors of tumors.
