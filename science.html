The Science

TriCurin

TriCurin is composed of plant derived components designated as GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe) by the FDA. It has a unique composition with one compound serving as a main "active" agent and two others as a "transport platform" for drug delivery.

To overcome the inherent problems with delivery of plant polyphenols, we devised a new approach, selecting plant molecules that chemically arrange themselves in a manner that not only enhances each other's biologic functions, but also improves their stability.

The unique ratio of components in TriCurin composition is based on thorough research and results in synergetic effect (patent pending).

In preclinical studies, TriCurin inhibits the expression of the viral oncoproteins E6 &E7, which is essential for the proliferation of HPV-infected cells, and increases the expression of P53 & RB, potent suppressors of tumors. In addition, TriCurin has shown to be non-toxic to healthy tissue.

Program: T-HPV 001

Current Development
